Work Experience

Data Analyst - Buhler Ltd., Buhler Group | Bangkok, Thailand (May 2019 - Aug 2019)

Investigated and experimented on the Cambodian paddy purity to discover the visually distinguishable features. Performed the purity resolution experiment – filtrating, cleaning and labeling 3 different paddies with total 10180, 6299, and 6129 samples respectively. Generated statistical analysis on grain’s geometrical characteristic such as width, height and aspect ratio and tested on purity resolving using confusion matrix. Achieved the accuracy respectively 98.40%, 98.73% and 98.78% with the test on purity resolving using confusion matrix. Verified efficiency of the machine vision technology to test the raw rice grain’s purity; with all results still in use.
